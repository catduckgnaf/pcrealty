Our Areas of Unique Expertise

Historic & Old Homes

We have a passion for the historic homes that define New England's charm. We understand the specific considerations that come with older properties, from preservation guidelines and zoning laws to the realities of knob-and-tube wiring and slate roofs. We know how to market their unique character and help buyers see their potential.

Investment Flips & Rehab Projects

As investment brokers, we excel at identifying properties with strong "fix-and-flip" potential. We help our clients analyze a property's "after repair value" (ARV), estimate renovation costs, and connect them with trusted contractors to turn a diamond in the rough into a profitable asset.

Why Complexity is Our Strength

  • Creative Problem Solving: Our background as engineers means we are natural problem-solvers. We excel at finding creative solutions to the financial, structural, and legal hurdles that can arise with unique properties.
  • Accurate Valuation: Valuing a unique property requires more than just running comps. We dig deeper, assessing the intrinsic value of historic features, the potential for renovation, and the specific market demand for non-traditional homes.
  • A Network of Specialists: From historic preservation consultants to specialized contractors and lenders, we have a network of experts ready to tackle any challenge a complex listing presents.
  • Unlocking Hidden Value: We believe that the most unique properties often hold the most potential. Whether it's a historic landmark, a multi-family conversion, or a property needing a complete overhaul, we have the vision and expertise to help you unlock its true value.
